1. 和值

大/小：如果开奖号码总和大于810，则为“大”；小于810为“小”；等于810为“和”，退还本金。
单/双：如果开奖号码总和或其尾数为1、3、5、7、9，则为“单”；为0、2、4、6、8，则为“双”。

2. 和值大小单双
如果开奖号码总和大于810则为“大”；小于810为“小”；如果其尾数为1、3、5、7、9为“单”；为0、2、4、6、8为“双”；等于810为“和”，退还本金。
大小与单双组合产生「大单」、「小单」、「大双」、「小双」四种组合。
例如：开奖号码总和为811；投注「大单」则中奖，因为811是“大”也是“单”。

3. 前多/后多
开奖号码中，号码01-40视为“前”，41-80视为“后”。
如果01-40的数量多于41-80，则投注「前多」中奖；反之，投注「后多」中奖；数量相等则退还本金。

4. 五行
20个开奖号码的总和分为五个区间，分别为：金（210-695）、木（696-763）、水（764-856）、火（857-924）、土（925-1410）。
例如：开奖号码总和为693；若投注「金」，则中奖，因为693属于“金”区间（210-695）。

5. 球号

B/S: If the sum of winning numbers is > 810 this is Big; < 810 this is Small; 810 as Tie, refund.
O/E: If the sum of winning numbers is or has an ending number of 1, 3, 5, 7, 9 this is Odd; 0, 2, 4, 6, 8 this is Even.

2. Sum Pass
If the sum of winning numbers is > 810 this is Big; < 810 this is Small; If the sum of winning numbers is or has an ending number of 1, 3, 5, 7, 9 this is Odd; 0, 2, 4, 6, 8 this is Even.; 810 as Tie, will refund. Big/Small, Odd/Even combo generate「Big Odd」,「Small Odd」,「Big Even」,「Small Even」, 4 combo.
E.g. Sum of winning numbers is 811; your bet is 「Big Odd」, this is a win because 811 is both big and odd and you win if you bet on Big Odd.

3. More First/Last
The winning numbers of 01-40 are regarded as First; and 41-80 are regarded as Last. So if the numbers between the range of 01-40 are more than the numbers between the range 41-80 betting on「More First」this is a win; if 41-80 is more than 01-40 betting on「More Last」this is a win; Equal will refund.

4. 5 Elements
The sum of the 20 winning numbers are arranged in 5 parts and named as Gold (210-695), Wood (696-763), Water (764-856), Fire (857-924), Earth (925-1410). E.g. If the sum of winning numbers is 693; You bet is「Gold」, this is win because the number 693 is included in Gold (210-695).
