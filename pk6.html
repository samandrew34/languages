龙/虎：
若前一个数字大于后一个数字，则为龙；反之为虎（1最小，6最大）。
第1局龙/虎：第1位与第6位比较
第2局龙/虎：第2位与第5位比较
第3局龙/虎：第3位与第4位比较
2. 定位玩法
选择第1位至第5位中的任意位置。
从1至6中任选一个数字。
若所选位置的开奖数字与下注数字一致，即为中奖。
例：开奖号码：1,* ,* ,* ,* , * ；下注第1位选数字1，则视为中奖。 english below Dragon/Tiger:
If the former number is bigger than the latter, it is considered Dragon; otherwise, it is Tiger (1 is the smallest, 6 is the biggest).
1st Dragon/Tiger:1st vs. 6th compete.
2nd Dragon/Tiger:2nd vs. 5th compete.
3rd Dragon/Tiger 3rd vs. 4th compete.
2. Fixed Place
Select places from 1st to 5th.
Select one number from any 1 to 6.
If the selected number and the winning number in the chosen place match, it is considered a win.
Example: Winning numbers: 1,* ,* ,* ,* , * ; Bet on 1st place with number 1, and it is considered a win.
