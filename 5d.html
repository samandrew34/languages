1 1. 总和 - 龙/虎/领带
B/S Sum:
五个中奖号码总和≥23为大，≤22为小。例如：中奖号码为2、3、9、4、5，则五个中奖号码总和为：2+3+9+4+5=23为大。中奖号码为2、3、1、4、5，则五个中奖号码总和为：2+3+1+4+5=15为小.
•O/E Sum:
当五个中奖号码的总和以 1、3、5、7、9 结尾时，即为奇数，例如 2+3+9+4+5= 23。结尾数字 (3) 使得该总和为奇数.当五个中奖号码的总和以 0、2、4、6、8 结尾时，即为偶数 例如 2+3+2+4+5=16，结尾数字 (6) 使得该总和为偶数.
•龙/虎/领带:
当第一球的中奖数字 > 第五球时，为龙。如果第一球 < 第五球，为虎。如果第一球 = 第五球，为平局。例如 7, 4, 2, 5, 1 - 第一球 (7) > 第五球 (1)，龙获胜。0, 1, 8, 3, 9 - 第一球 (0) < 第五球 (9)，虎获胜。1, 3, 7, 0, 1 - 第一球 (1) = 第五球 (1)，平局获胜。

•选择 1 个位置并投注 1 个元素:
从 0-9 的数字范围内，选择一个将出现在 1 号球、2 号球、3 号球、4 号球或 5 号球上的数字。如果您选择 6 出现在 2 号球上，并且抽奖结果显示 6 出现在 2 号球上，则您获胜
B/S:
当一球中奖数字≥5时为“大”；当一球中奖数字≤4时为“小”。
O/E:
当球上的中奖数字为 1、3、5、7、9 时，为“奇数”；当球上的中奖数字以 0、2、4、6、8 结尾时，为“偶数”。
P/C:
当球上的中奖号码为 1、2、3、5、7 时，为“素数”；当球上的中奖号码为 0、4、6、8、9 时，为“合数”。
3. 固定名次1~5名
从第1、2、3、4、5位中选择一个数字进行下注，当您选择的号码和中奖号码在同一位时，您就赢了。 例如：投注方案：3,-,-,-,-；中奖号码：3,*,*,*,*，即为中奖。您选择 3 出现在第一球上，而中奖号码在第一球上也有 3。即为中奖。
4. 独特的前 3 名、中 3 名、最后 3 名
前 3: 目标为 [1st, 2nd, 3rd] 个球。
中 3: 目标是 [第 2、3、4 个] 球。
最后 3: 目标为 [3, 4, 5] 个球。

投注类型:
托克 （三条）：当 3 个数字相同时就会出现这种情况。 例如：中奖号码 1、1、1、*、*。投注「首3个TOAK」，视为中奖。
条纹: 当 3 个数字相关或排成一行时就会发生这种情况。 例：中奖号码9、1、0、*、*，投注「首3连胜」，视为中奖。
一对: 当总共 3 个中奖数字中 1 个数字出现 2 次时，就会出现这种情况。 例：中奖号码0、3、0、*、*，投注「首三对」，视为中奖。
半连胜: 3 个不同的数字，2 个相关或连续的数字。 例：中奖号码0、1、3、*、*，投注「首3连胜」，视为中奖。

1. Sum - Dragon/Tiger/Tie
B/S Sum:
When the sum of the five winning drawn numbers is ≥ 23, this is Big, when it is ≤ 22 - this is Small. Eg. If the winning numbers are 2, 3, 9, 4, 5, the sum of the 5 winning numbers is: 2+3+9+4+5=23, this is big. If the winning numbers are 2, 3, 1, 4, 5, the sum of the 5 winning numbers is: 2+3+1+4+5=15, this is small.
O/E Sum:
When the sum of the five winning drawn numbers ends with 1, 3, 5, 7, 9 this is Odd Eg. 2+3+9+4+5= 23. The ending number (3) makes this sum Odd. When the sum of the five winning drawn numbers ends with 0, 2, 4, 6, 8 this is Even Eg. 2+3+2+4+5=16, The ending number (6) makes this sum Even.
Dragon/Tiger/Tie: When the winning number on the 1st Ball > 5th Ball, this is dragon. If the 1st Ball < 5th Ball, this is a tiger. If 1st Ball = 5th Ball, this is Tie. Eg. 7, 4, 2, 5, 1 - The 1st Ball (7) is > than the 5th Ball (1), Dragon wins. 0, 1, 8, 3, 9 - The 1st Ball (0) is < than the 5th Ball (9), Tiger wins. 1, 3, 7, 0, 1 - The 1st Ball (1) is = to the 5th Ball (1), Tie wins.
2. 1st~5th
Select 1 Place & Bet 1 Element:
From the range of numbers 0-9, you choose a number that will appear either on Ball 1, Ball 2, Ball 3, Ball 4, Ball 5. If you choose 6 to appear on Ball 2 and the draw results has the number 6 appearing on Ball 2, you win.
B/S:
When the winning draw number on a ball is ≥ 5 this is "Big"; When the winning number on a ball is ≤ 4 this is "Small."
O/E:
When the winning draw number on a ball is 1, 3, 5, 7, 9 this is "Odd"; When the winning draw number on a ball ends with 0, 2, 4, 6, 8 this is "Even."
P/C:
When the winning draw number on a ball is 1, 2, 3, 5, 7 this is "Prime"; When the winning draw number on a ball is 0, 4, 6, 8, 9 this is "Composite."

Select a number from 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th place to form a bet. When your selected number and winning draw number are in the same place, you win. Eg. Bet plan: 3,-,-,-,-; Winning No.: 3,*,*,*,* as a win. You select 3 to appear on the 1st Ball, and the winning drawn number also has 3 on the 1st Ball. This wins. 4. Unique First 3, Mid 3, Last 3

Betting Types:
TOAK (Three of a Kind): This occurs when 3 numbers are the same. E.g. Winning number 1, 1, 1, *, *. Bet「First 3 TOAK」, as win.
Streak: This occurs when 3 numbers are related or are in a row. E.g. Winning number 9, 1, 0, *, *. Bet「First 3 Streak」, as win.
Pair: This occurs when 1 number out of the total 3 winning drawn numbers occur 2x. E.g. Winning number 0, 3, 0, *, *. Bet「First 3 Pair」, as win.[First 3 Streak」, as win.
Half Streak: 3 different numbers, 2 numbers related or in row. E.g. Winning number 0, 1, 3, *, *. Bet「First 3 Half Streak」, as win.
Mixed 6: 3 different & unrelated numbers or not in row. E.g. Winning number 0, 3, 5, *, *. Bet「First 3 Mixed 6」, as win.
